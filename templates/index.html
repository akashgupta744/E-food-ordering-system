Over the years, the food plaza concept spread globally, becoming a ubiquitous feature in malls, airports, and urban centers. Fast food chains, local eateries, and international cuisine outlets frequently join forces to provide consumers with a broad spectrum of dining options in a communal setting. Food plazas have not only become a convenient solution for busy individuals but also contribute to the social aspect of dining out, fostering a shared space for people to gather and enjoy meals together.
